FAQ

  • If I'm traveling, when should I arrive?
    The weekend kicks off with dinner on Friday May 30 at 6pm. If you'd like to come Saturday instead, events start at 11am.
  • If I'm traveling, when should I head out?
    Dinner on Saturday will be our last official event, but there's lots to do Sunday if you stick around. Check out our map of stuff to do in the city, or join for a day trip to Rarify's warehouse in Lebanon, PA. We also might try to do an architectural walking tour on Sunday.
  • What if I only want to come to some stuff?
    That's cool. When you RSVP, let us know what you'd like to come to— we'll need some commitments in advance for reservations and deposits.
  • What else is there to do in Philly?
    We're glad you asked. We have a map for that.
  • What's a nanosummit?
    A loose group of creatives, technologists, and architects called Neuros have been going to cool places together for years. While we haven't done a true Neurosummit in a few years, this is an opportunity to meet up on a smaller scale. Thus, a Nannosummit.

Hope you can come!
